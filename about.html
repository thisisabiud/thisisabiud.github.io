More about KASS

Who is the owner?

The school is owned by Seventh-day Adventist Church in Eastern Tanzania Conference(ETC).

Where?

Located 10 Kilometers from Morogoro town along the main road Morogoro - Dar Es Salaam, between Tafori and Kingolwira village.

However,

Those who will join this school must follow all rules and regulations keeping all God's commandments including the fourth commandment concerning keeping the sabbath on Saturday as the holy bible sayth (exodus 20:8-11)

Enrollment criteria

Everyone is welcome to join this community regardless of race, sex and religious denomination.

It is a boarding school and accepts all sex, follows all the rules of The Ministry of Education, Science, and Technology of The united Republic of Tanzania.
